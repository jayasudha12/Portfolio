Airline Reservation System

The Airline Reservation System is a Java-based application designed to facilitate the booking and management of airline tickets. The system provides a user-friendly interface for customers to search for available flights, reserve seats, and manage their bookings. It also offers administrative functionalities for airline staff to manage flight schedules, seat availability, and customer reservations.

E Waste Management System

The E-Waste Management System is a Java-based application designed to facilitate the collection, recycling, and disposal of electronic waste (e-waste). The system provides a user-friendly interface for both consumers and recycling centers to manage e-waste collection requests, track inventory, and ensure proper disposal practices. It aims to promote environmental sustainability by encouraging responsible disposal of electronic devices.
